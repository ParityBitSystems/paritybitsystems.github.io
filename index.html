About Michael Peacock

Michael has served as a consultant CTO for almost three years, before which he spent time as CTO for a technology investment company, lead web developer on an electric vehicle telematics project and as MD of a small web design & development agency.

He holds a degree in Software Engineering from the University of Durham, and is a Zend Certified Engineer.

With a wealth of experience in software development, Michael is the author of 8 published books, has spoken at a number of user groups and conferences both nationally and internationally, and co-organises the PHP North East and Laravel North East user groups.
